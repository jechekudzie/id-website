Wedding Photography

Golder Hour Wedding

Project Info

  • Client: Sarah & David
  • Location: Bali, Indonesia
  • Date: 20 February 2023
  • View the project

Golden Hour is a photographer’s dream – that brief period just before sunset when the light is soft, warm, and perfect for capturing magical moments.

For Sarah and David’s wedding, held on the breathtaking beaches of Bali, this natural beauty set the scene for an unforgettable day of photography.

We worked closely with the couple and the wedding planner to ensure the ceremony and key moments coincided with the peak of Golden Hour.

Planning and Scouting

We scouted the venue in advance to identify the best spots for capturing the ceremony and portraits during Golden Hour.

The Equipment Setup

We prepared softbox lighting and reflectors to subtly enhance specific shots without overpowering the natural ambiance.

Capturing the Ceremony

We shot a mix of wide-angle views and intimate close-ups, ensuring the beautiful setting and emotions were captured.

Golden Hour Portraits

As the sun set, we transitioned to beachside portraits, using natural backlighting to create ethereal images.

Tools and software

  • Post-processing: Adobe Lightroom
  • Retouching: Adobe Photoshop
  • Image processing: Capture One Pro
  • Portable lighting: Godox AD200 Pro

We couldn’t have asked for more perfect photos! The timing, the light, and the attention to detail were just amazing. Each photo captured the essence of our day so beautifully.

— Sarah & David

With careful preparation this Golden Hour wedding became a highlight of the season.

The ceremony was scheduled to begin one hour before sunset, allowing us to capture the vows with the soft afternoon light.

The light was perfect, creating golden halos around them, especially when framed against the ocean horizon.

Key Highlights of the Work Process:

  • Perfectly timing the golden hour for optimal lighting
  • Comprehensive location scouting to maximize scenic beauty
  • Seamless integration of natural light and studio gear
  • Capturing authentic emotions in a candid, unscripted style
